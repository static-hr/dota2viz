info
Data collected from joindota.
Nodes represent teams. Directed edges (connections) represent match outcome.
Example: Team Winner wins over team Loser 3:1. This will result in
edge Loser Winner with weight 3 (Loser gives 3 points to Winner) and
edge Winner Loser with weight 1 (Winner gives 1 point to Loser).
